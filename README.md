# FinTwin: Your Intelligent Financial Simulator

## 1. Problem Statement
Traditional finance apps are passive; they only record what has already happened. Users often struggle to make informed decisions about large purchases, recurring expenses, or the trade-offs between cash payments and EMIs, leading to avoidable financial stress and missed saving opportunities.

## 2. The Solution
**FinTwin** is an advanced financial simulation web application designed to act as your digital "twin." By combining expense tracking with predictive simulation, FinTwin helps you run "what-if" scenarios directly in your browser before you spend.

## 3. Key Features
* **Intelligent Financial Sandbox:** Run simulations to see how a potential purchase impacts your future savings.
* **Smart Purchase Advisor:** Get data-driven recommendations on whether to use **Cash or EMI** to optimize your long-term cash flow.
* **Marketplace Price Comparison:** Compare similar items across different marketplaces to ensure you always get the best price.
* **Dynamic Financial Profile:** Adjust your **income and savings goals** over time as your financial situation changes.
* **Activity Logging:** View your "Sandbox" history in the **Recent Activities** log to track your spending habits.
* **Personalized Profile:** Track your expenses and manage your digital identity with custom profile photo uploads.

## 4. Tech Stack
* **Frontend:**  HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
* **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js
* **Database:** MongoDB (via MongoDB Atlas)
* **Deployment:** Render

## 5. How the Simulator Works
1. **Access:** Navigate to the FinTwin web portal and log in to your secure account.
2. **Input:** Enter the item you are considering, its price, and your current financial status.
3. **Analyze:** The FinTwin simulator evaluates the purchase against your profile metrics in real-time.
4. **Suggest:** Receive actionable advice on **payment methods** and price alternatives.
5. **Log:** All simulations are saved in your personal **Recent Activities** for future review.

---

### Deployment Note
This application is deployed using **Render** for the backend service and **MongoDB Atlas** for the database, ensuring high availability and secure data management.
